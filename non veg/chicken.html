Chicken Curry

← Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Process

  1. Sauté Onions: Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, and fry until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sauté for a minute.
  2. Cook Tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  3. Cook Chicken: Add the chicken pieces, mix well, and cook for 10-15 minutes until tender.
  4. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle garam masala, cook for another 2 minutes, and garnish with coriander. Serve hot with rice or roti!
