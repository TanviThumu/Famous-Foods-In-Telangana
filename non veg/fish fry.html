Fish Fry

← Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (like tilapia or pomfret)
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ cup rice flour or besan (gram flour)
  • Oil for frying

Process

  1. Marinate Fish: Mix lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Coat the fish fillets with this mixture and let it marinate for 15-30 minutes.
  2. Coat with Flour: Dredge the marinated fish in rice flour or besan for a crispy coating.
  3. Fry: Heat oil in a pan and fry the coated fish until golden and crispy on both sides (about 3-4 minutes per side).
  4. Serve: Drain excess oil and serve hot with lemon wedges and chutney!
