Liver Fry

← Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 500g liver (chicken or goat, cleaned and sliced)
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Process

  1. Sauté Onions: Heat oil in a pan, add sliced onions, and fry until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; sauté for a minute.
  2. Cook Liver: Add liver, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook on medium heat until the liver is cooked through (about 10-15 minutes).
  3. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti!
