Mutton Haleem

← Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton (with bones)
  • 1 cup wheat or barley (soaked)
  • ¼ cup lentils (mixed)
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon ghee/oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Process

  1. Cook Meat: Pressure cook mutton with ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and salt until tender.
  2. Cook Grains: Simultaneously, cook soaked wheat, barley, and lentils until soft.
  3. Blend and Mix: Blend the cooked grains and add them to the cooked mutton. Stir in garam masala and cook on low heat.
  4. Garnish and Serve: Fry sliced onions in ghee, mix into the haleem, and garnish with fresh coriander. Serve hot!
