Panner Biryani

← Back to Home

Ingredients

    For Marinating Paneer:

  • 200 grams paneer (cubed)
  • ½ cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste

    • For Cooking Rice:

  • 1½ cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • Salt to taste
  • Water

    • For Biryani:

  • 2 medium onions (sliced)
  • 1 medium tomato (chopped)
  • 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon mint leaves (chopped)
  • 3 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Saffron strands (soaked in 2 tablespoons of milk)
  • Fried onions (optional for garnish)
  • ½ cup water

Process

    Step 1:Marinate the Paneer

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, ginger-garlic paste, and salt.
  2. Add paneer cubes to the marinade and coat them well.
  3. Let the paneer marinate for at least 20-30 minutes.
