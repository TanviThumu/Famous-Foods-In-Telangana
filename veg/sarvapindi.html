Ingredients

  • Rice flour - 1 cup
  • Chana dal - 2 tbsp (soaked)
  • Green chilies - 2, chopped
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Process

  1. Mix flour: Mix all the ingredients in a bowl with water to form a thick dough.
  2. Shape the dough: Flatten the dough on a greased pan to a thin circle.
  3. Cook: Cook on low flame with a little oil until both sides are golden brown.
  4. Serve: Serve hot with chutney.
