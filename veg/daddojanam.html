Daddojanam

← Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice (cooled)
  • 1½ cups curd (yogurt)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Process

  1. Mix Rice and Curd: In a bowl, combine the cooled rice with curd and salt.
  2. Prepare Tempering: Heat oil or ghee in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until aromatic.
  3. Combine: Pour the tempering over the rice-curd mixture and mix gently.
  4. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with coriander leaves if desired. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
