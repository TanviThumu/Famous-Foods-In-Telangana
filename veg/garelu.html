Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (soaked for 4-5 hours)
  • 2-3 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • Curry leaves (optional)
  • Salt to tastec
  • Oil for deep frying

Process

  1. Grind Batter: Drain soaked urad dal and grind into a smooth batter with minimal water. Add salt, green chilies, cumin seeds, onions, and curry leaves.
  2. Shape Vadas: Wet your hand, take a small portion of the batter, shape into a donut, and make a hole in the center.
  3. Fry: Heat oil in a deep pan. Carefully slide the vadas into the hot oil and fry until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve: Drain excess oil and serve hot with chutney or sambar. Enjoy!
